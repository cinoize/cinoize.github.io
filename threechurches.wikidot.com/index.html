Why we are 3 Churches One Location!

About Usâ€¦

  • We are three separate and autonomous Christian congregations that share the same facilities in Coon Rapids, MN.
  • We are inter-denominational, inter-racial, inter-generational, inter-cultural, and inter-connected through our Christian faith.
  • We are Olive Branch Lutheran, Waters of Life Outreach and Filipino American Christian churches.
  • We are a faith community that is marked by ingenuity, diversity and tolerance.
  • We are Asian, European, African and American.
  • We are Lutheran, Baptist and Pentecostal.
  • We are passionate dreamers and eager workers of God's word and will.
  • We are "all for one and one for all" in Jesus Christ!
