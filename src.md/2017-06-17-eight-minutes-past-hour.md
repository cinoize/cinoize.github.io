In hour 4 he reaches the difficult sandy realm of&nbsp;<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seker" style="background: none; color: rgb(11 , 0 , 128);" target="_blank" title="Seker">Sokar</a>, the underworld hawk deity, where he encounters dark zig zag pathways which he has to negotiate, being dragged on a snake-boat.
