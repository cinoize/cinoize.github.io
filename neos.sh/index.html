What is Donu?

Donito is made up of two components. First, there is a platform that allows you to accept popular cryptocurrencies in your fundraising campaigns. You can also accept and hold the native cryptocurrency, DONU, which offers additional incentives.

Read our whitepaper

We designed a platform with community growth in mind.

Donito is a non-profit platform designed to support users and DONU token holders. The small fee we charge on transactions goes directly towards buying DONU tokens at market value and optionally burning them.

Plus, when your supporters send you DONU tokens, you’ll pay even lower fees!

donu FAQ

How do I migrate from my Neos wallet to Donu?

Migrating from your Neos wallet to Donu can be done as easily as copying your wallet.dat file from your Neos data directory into your new Donu data directory. We suggest reading this knowledgebase article for in-depth instructions.

What are the blockchain specs?

Block Spacing - 60 seconds
Block Finder Reward - 0.4 DONU
Masternode Reward - 0.4 DONU
Platform Emissions - 0.2 DONU
Network Port - 26380
RPC Port - 26381
Masternode collateral - 5000 DONU
Minimum input size for staking - 500 DONU
Minimum staking age - 30 Days
Maximum staking age - 60 days

What can Donu network tokens be used for?

Donu network tokens (DONU) can be used on or off of the Donito platform to make fast and easy payments and donations. Owning DONU will additionally provide a substantial discount to those using the platform, and access to features on the platform that may be require DONU.

Where can I view Donu blocks and transactions?

You can visit the cryptoID Donu block explorer.
