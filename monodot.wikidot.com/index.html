My Child Pushchair & Car Seat-Monodot

My Child Versa 2 in 1 Pushchair - Black Monodot

The Versa is a very modern, stylish 2 in 1 Pushchair suitable from birth to 4 years. It can be used with a carrycot to become a 3 in 1 combi if required. With front swivel wheels and large rear wheels the Versa is extremely easy to manoeuver. The aluminium frame makes this very light weight and with an adjustable handle bar is adaptable for both mum and dad!!

The seat unit can be used either facing parents or facing forward. This means you can have your newborn facing you to encourage communication, and your older child can face forward and see the world!!

Features:

  1. Fully reclining seat unit suitable from birth
  2. Comes complete with hood and raincover
  3. 5 point safety harness
  4. Front or rear facing seat unit
  5. Easy folding to a very compact size
  6. Removeable bumper bar

My Child Versa Car Seat-Monodot

Part Of the fabulously verstile 3 in 1 travel system, to take your baby comfortably from birth to 4 years old. Great manoeverability, thanks to the combination of front swivel wheels and large rear wheels. Surprisingly lightweight, thanks to the hi-tech aluminium frame. Adjustable height handle with ergonomicaly designed soft grips.

Features:

  1. Group 0+ Car Seat fits easily onto pushchair chassis
  2. Suitable from birth to 13kgs
  3. 5 point harness for comfort and security
  4. Headhugger for extra support
  5. Removeable hood
  6. Side impact protection
  7. Weight 2.75Kgs
  • Manufacturer: My Child
  • RRP: £79.99
