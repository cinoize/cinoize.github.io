Power Meets Convenience

Leave the heaviest technical aspects of server management to us, while still getting a lot of freedom. DV Managed is professional-grade VPS hosting with the ease of Plesk or cPanel.

  • Shift between a wide range of resource plans.

  • Comes with a robust LAMP stack, root access, developer tools: Everything youd expect from a highly flexible managed VPS solution.

  • With the latest versions of Plesk or cPanel & WHM, you get a best-in-class control panel with streamlined configuration and numerous automated features.

  • Available with up to 64GB of RAM with multi-core Intel Xeon processors and SSD storage. Burstable resources help isolate from noisy neighbors.

Features of Your DV Server

  • 99.99% uptime guaranteed

  • All SSD drives

    Weve got solid-state technology at every turn, so your data and databases perform faster and more reliably than with a clockwork of moving HDD parts.

  • On-demand scaling
