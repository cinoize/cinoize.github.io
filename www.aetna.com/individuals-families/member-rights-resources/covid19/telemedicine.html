    Telemedicine is a smart, safe choice

    You can meet with a doctor by phone or video chat 24/7 from the comfort of home, while protecting yourself and others. So if youre sick and need care right away, telemedicine may be a great option for you.

    Get started with virtual care for $0 copay*

    Get started with virtual care for $0 copay*

    Telemedicine should be your first line of defense for non-emergency care to help limit potential exposure to the coronavirus. Thats why weve changed our policies with providers so they can deliver care to you virtually.

    Take the first step:

    Review your plan coverage

    Different plans feature different coverage and costs. So take the time to review your plan details today. Participating plans are offering a $0 copay* for telemedicine visits until June 4, 2020 to help you stay healthy.

    Three ways to get support:

    Call your primary care physician

    Many doctors are treating patients through virtual care, such as over the phone and live videoconferencing. Call your doctor to see if theyre participating in telemedicine. Since they already know your medical history, theyre a great first choice.

    Use Teladoc services

    If your doctor isnt available and you need care today, or after hours or on weekends, you can meet with a doctor by phone or video 24/7. Get access to Teladoc through theAetna HealthSM app.

    Find virtual care providers for your state

    If your doctor isn't offering telemedicine or youre just looking for more options, you can also find many providers who are offering virtual care in your state.

    What services are covered under telemedicine?

    What services are covered under telemedicine?

    You get access to a variety of providers and services to get quality care at any time, for many everyday needs.

    Many options are now covered, including:

    • Routine care
    • Urgent medical care
    • Behavioral health
    • Physical, speech, and occupational therapies
    • Teledentistry, and more

    Get quality care at home

    Get quality care at home

    Coronavirus is spread from an infected person to others through the air by coughing and sneezing, as well as through close personal contact. Thats why were being asked to keep a social distance to help prevent its spread.

    With telehealth, you can meet with a doctor by phone or video chat without having to go in to their office. Often you can seek telehealth treatment after hours or on weekends. So, if youre sick and need to see a doctor, or need care right away, it may be a great option for you. Its fast, easy and it can give you peace of mind.

    Telehealth visits for $0 copay

    Telehealth visits for $0 copay

    Telehealth isnt just aconvenient way to access care, it can also help you avoid exposure to the COVID-19 virus. To support you in obtaining your care via telehealth,were offering a $0 copay for telehealth visits at this time.

    Well waive costs for all telehealth visits for:

    • All Medicare plans, including group, individual, Medicare-Medicaid plans and dual SNPs
    • Contracted, network Medicare providers who deliver virtual care, such as live videoconferencing

    Until further notice, Aetna will offer zero co-pay telemedicine visits for any reason to all Individual and Group Medicare Advantage members. Aetna Medicare Advantage members should use telemedicine as their first line of defense to limit potential exposure in physician offices. Cost sharing will be waived for all Teladoc virtual visits. Cost sharing will also be waived for real-time virtual visits offered by in-network providers (live video conferencing or telephone-only telemedicine services). Medicare Advantage members may use telemedicine for any reason, not just COVID-19 diagnosis.

    Helping you get started

    Helping you get started

    If youre interested in telehealth services, just contact your provider. Theyll let you know how they can see you by virtual care services, which include videoconferencing and over-the-phone care.

    Just keep in mind that if you take advantage of telehealth visits, youmay have a littlelonger wait timethan usual to speak with a doctor.
